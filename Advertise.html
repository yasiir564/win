Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about advertising with TikDownloader.

What types of ads can I place on TikDownloader?

We offer multiple ad formats including banner ads, video ads, native integrations, and sponsored content. Our team can help you select the best format based on your marketing objectives and target audience.

How much does advertising cost?

Our advertising packages start at $150 per month for banner ads. Pricing varies based on placement, format, duration, and targeting options. We offer flexible options to accommodate various budgets and can create custom packages tailored to your specific needs.

Who will see my advertisements?

Your ads will be seen by our global audience of over 5 million monthly visitors. Our users primarily consist of TikTok enthusiasts aged 16-34 who are engaged with social media content. We offer geographical targeting options to help you reach specific markets if desired.

How do I track the performance of my ads?

We provide all advertisers with access to our analytics dashboard where you can monitor impressions, clicks, engagement rates, and conversion data in real-time. Monthly performance reports are also included with all advertising packages.

What is the minimum contract period?

We offer flexible contract terms starting from one month. However, we provide discounted rates for longer-term commitments of 3, 6, or 12 months. There are no automatic renewals, and you can adjust your campaign at the end of each contract period.
